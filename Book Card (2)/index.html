Dune

Novel by Frank Herbet

Set in the far future amidst a sprawling feudal interstellar empire where planetary dynasties are controlled by noble houses that owe an allegiance to the imperial House Corrino, Dune tells the story of young Paul Atreides (the heir to the Duke Leto Atreides and heir to the House of Atreides) as he and his family accept control of the desert planet Arrakis...
